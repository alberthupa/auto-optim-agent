# The Pocket Spellbook

The pocket spellbook lists three canonical spells. Feather Fall slows descent
near cliffs. Warm Light kindles a small lantern. Silent Step muffles footsteps
for a short time. Each spell costs one charge.

Spells regenerate at dawn. The book itself was copied from the Larger Grimoire
in the city of Aldreth.
