[00:00] Interviewer: When does memory-ingest feel trustworthy in practice?
[00:12] Eva: When the raw capture stays visible and the consolidated note
points back to it. I need both.
[00:31] Interviewer: What breaks first when that relation is weak?
[00:39] Eva: Quote attribution. If a quote moves into a summary with no
source provenance, I stop trusting it.
[00:57] Interviewer: Anything else you watch for?
[01:04] Eva: Near-duplicates. One reviewable note plus durable concept notes
works. Five almost-identical notes make retrieval worse.
