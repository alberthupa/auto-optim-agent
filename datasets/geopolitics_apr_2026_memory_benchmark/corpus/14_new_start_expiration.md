# New START expiration and arms-control uncertainty

The expiration of New START in February 2026 is used in this corpus as a marker of broader erosion in strategic restraint. It matters not because it dominates every headline, but because it removes a stabilizing framework at a time of already elevated confrontation.

## Key points

- The treaty had limited strategic nuclear weapons between the United States and Russia.
- Its expiration added uncertainty to an already unstable security environment.
- The event fits the corpus theme of weakening guardrails in great-power rivalry.
