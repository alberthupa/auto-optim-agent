# Ceasefire fragility in Lebanon

Lebanon appears in the dataset as a reminder that formal ceasefire language does not guarantee operational calm. Strikes, casualties, and retaliatory operations continue to test the durability of any nominal de-escalation.

## Key points

- Israeli strikes and Hezbollah operations continued to fuel instability.
- Local violence persisted despite ceasefire talk.
- This mirrors the broader corpus pattern of weak enforcement and low trust.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note creates useful parallels with the failed Easter ceasefire in the Russia-Ukraine file.
