# Export controls as core statecraft

Export controls are portrayed here as a central geopolitical instrument, especially in the competition over AI compute and high-end manufacturing capacity. They sit alongside tariffs and sanctions as one of the key tools of geoeconomic confrontation.

## Key points

- Controls on advanced chips shape access to computing power and industrial capability.
- They affect not just trade flows but strategic capacity.
- They also create second-order effects such as rerouting and allied coordination needs.
