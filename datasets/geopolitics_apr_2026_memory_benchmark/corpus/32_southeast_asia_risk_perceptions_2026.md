# Southeast Asian risk perceptions in 2026

Regional perceptions in Southeast Asia reflect overlapping anxieties about major-power behavior, maritime coercion, and economic vulnerability. The corpus uses these perceptions to show that regional actors do not experience global rivalry through only one lens.

## Key points

- Chinese behavior in the South China Sea remained a major concern.
- US leadership choices also shaped regional anxiety.
- Perception data helps explain why states may hedge rather than align neatly.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note is helpful for questions about regional hedging and mixed threat perception.
