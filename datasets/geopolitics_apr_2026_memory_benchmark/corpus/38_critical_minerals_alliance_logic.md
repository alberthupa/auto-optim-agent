# The alliance logic behind critical-minerals coordination

Critical-minerals coordination in the corpus is not portrayed as a mere commercial partnership. It has alliance logic because shared vulnerability in inputs can weaken both industrial output and defense readiness.

## Key points

- Coordination aims to reduce shared exposure to concentrated supply chains.
- Industrial resilience and military readiness are linked in this logic.
- The policy response is therefore strategic, not just commercial.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note should connect to minerals, semiconductors, and Western strategic alignment.
