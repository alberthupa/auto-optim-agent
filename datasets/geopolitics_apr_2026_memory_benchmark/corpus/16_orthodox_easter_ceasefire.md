# The Orthodox Easter ceasefire episode

The Easter ceasefire is a compact illustration of symbolic diplomacy without durable trust. Even though a pause was announced, both sides quickly accused the other of violations, turning the gesture into further evidence of how low confidence had become.

## Key points

- The ceasefire was brief and politically visible.
- Mutual accusations of violations followed quickly.
- The episode reinforced the pattern of diplomacy without dependable implementation.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note rewards memory systems that capture both the existence of the ceasefire and the fact that it failed to build trust.
