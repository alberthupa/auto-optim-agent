# Taiwan's porcupine strategy in practice

The porcupine strategy is not just a slogan in this benchmark. It refers to a defensive logic based on resilience, mobility, and asymmetric denial rather than prestige systems designed for mirror-image competition.

## Key points

- The approach emphasizes systems that are cheaper, mobile, and harder to neutralize.
- It is designed to complicate invasion or coercion rather than match China symmetrically.
- The concept links doctrine, procurement, and strategic signaling.
