# How Middle East instability spills into Europe

European consequences of Middle East instability in this corpus include energy strain, sanctions dilemmas, and alliance attention shifts. The point is to show that geographic distance does not prevent operational entanglement.

## Key points

- Energy disruptions create direct economic and political pressure in Europe.
- Sanctions and waiver debates become harder when markets are under stress.
- NATO attention can be pulled toward maritime security and burden-sharing questions.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note supports cross-regional reasoning between Hormuz, sanctions, and NATO.
