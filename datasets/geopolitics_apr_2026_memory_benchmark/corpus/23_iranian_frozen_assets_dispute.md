# The dispute over frozen Iranian assets

Frozen Iranian assets become a focal point in the corpus because they sit at the intersection of sanctions, leverage, and negotiation credibility. Competing public claims about whether assets would be released add another layer of uncertainty.

## Key points

- Iran linked asset access to wider negotiations and shipping issues.
- The US publicly denied agreeing to release those assets.
- The dispute highlights how negotiation narratives can diverge sharply.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note is useful for testing whether the system retains contested claims and counterclaims rather than flattening them into a false consensus.
