# NATO adaptation, long-range strike, and new technology

NATO's challenge in this corpus is not only spending more. It is adapting faster, including through long-range conventional strike capabilities and more rapid integration of emerging technology into military planning and operations.

## Key points

- Adaptation speed matters alongside raw defense budgets.
- Long-range strike and emerging technologies are part of alliance modernization.
- The alliance is being asked to handle multiple theaters and problem sets at once.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note allows benchmark questions that separate spending targets from actual strategic adaptation.
