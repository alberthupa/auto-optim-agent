# A framework for strategic chokepoints

Strategic chokepoints in this corpus are not limited to narrow passages on a map. They also include concentrated processing capacity, fragile infrastructure links, and supply nodes whose disruption can reshape policy across regions.

## Key points

- Hormuz is the leading energy chokepoint example.
- Rare-earth processing concentration is an industrial chokepoint example.
- Critical communications infrastructure is a gray-zone chokepoint example.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note should become a concept hub linking energy, minerals, and infrastructure coercion.
