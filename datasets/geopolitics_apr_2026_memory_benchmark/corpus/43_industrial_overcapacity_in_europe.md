# Industrial overcapacity as a European strategic concern

European anxiety about Chinese industrial overcapacity appears in the corpus as more than a complaint about cheap imports. It is presented as a strategic concern because overcapacity can hollow out local industry and increase dependence in sensitive sectors.

## Key points

- The issue spans vehicles, solar, wind, and semiconductors.
- Strategic sectors are treated differently from ordinary consumer competition.
- This encourages procurement restrictions and tougher policy responses.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note supports questions that distinguish classic protectionism from economic security.
