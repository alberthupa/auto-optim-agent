# Taiwan's US arms procurement choices

Taiwan's procurement choices in the corpus reinforce the asymmetry logic. Systems like Javelin missiles and HIMARS are included not as a shopping list, but as examples of a denial-oriented defense posture.

## Key points

- Approved purchases included Javelin missiles and HIMARS.
- These systems fit a mobile and asymmetric approach.
- Procurement decisions therefore encode strategic doctrine.
