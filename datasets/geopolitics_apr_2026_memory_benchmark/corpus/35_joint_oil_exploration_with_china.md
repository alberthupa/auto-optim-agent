# Joint oil exploration with China as a tactical option

Discussion of joint exploration is included in the benchmark because it captures the logic of tactical accommodation under stress. An energy emergency can make compromise or partial reset attractive even when underlying territorial disputes remain unresolved.

## Key points

- Joint exploration became more appealing as the energy crunch deepened.
- Territorial and constitutional obstacles still made the idea politically difficult.
- The proposal therefore mixed pragmatism with strategic discomfort.

This note is useful for testing whether the memory system preserves both incentive and constraint in one answer.
