# AI compute competition as a geopolitical driver

The benchmark treats AI compute as a geopolitical driver because access to high-end processors shapes innovation, surveillance, military applications, and industrial competitiveness. That is why chip restrictions appear repeatedly across the corpus.

## Key points

- Advanced processors are tied to AI capability.
- Competition over compute is therefore tied to broader national power.
- Demand for restricted chips remains high because the stakes are strategic.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note lets the memory system demonstrate that it can connect AI to industrial policy and export controls.
