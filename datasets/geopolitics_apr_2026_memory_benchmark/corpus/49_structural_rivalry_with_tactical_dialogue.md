# Structural rivalry with tactical dialogue

One of the recurring benchmark patterns is that states can keep talking while still competing intensely. Tactical dialogue seeks stability and communication, but it does not erase structural rivalry over security, industry, and influence.

## Key points

- Paris summit preparation is one example of tactical dialogue.
- US-Iran talks in Islamabad are another example of contact without settlement.
- The pattern matters because diplomacy can signal process without solving underlying conflict.
