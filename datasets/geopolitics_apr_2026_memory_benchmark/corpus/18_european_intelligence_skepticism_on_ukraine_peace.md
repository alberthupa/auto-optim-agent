# European intelligence skepticism about Ukraine peace

Some European intelligence assessments in the corpus are pessimistic about a genuine settlement in 2026. The point is not simply that peace is hard, but that some diplomacy may be performative or tactical rather than a reliable path to de-escalation.

## Key points

- Intelligence reporting conveyed skepticism about a near-term peace deal.
- Some talks were viewed as theatrical rather than substantive.
- This skepticism contextualizes why ceasefires and negotiations remained fragile.

## Why this matters for the benchmark

This note helps test whether the memory system captures epistemic stance, not just event headlines.
